Indicator 11.2.3

global_indicators.11-2-3-title

The indicator shows the number of bee colonies in the district Treptow-Köpenick. A total of 240 beekeepers are registered, each taking care of an average of 4 colonies, i.e. a total of 960 bee colonies.
